How it works

1
Open any webpage
Navigate to any site — a Wikipedia article, a documentation page, a research paper.
2
Click the NotebookOS icon
The side panel opens. If you've been here before, your notes load instantly. First visit? You'll be asked to choose a notebook.
3
Write — it saves automatically
Notes autosave every 2.5 seconds after you stop typing. No save button needed.
4
Come back anytime
Revisit the same URL — notes are right there, exactly as you left them. No searching, no organizing.
